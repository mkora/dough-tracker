Simple Home Finance Application

Simple Home Finance Application calculates and displays statistic in tables and graphs on your spendings, earnings and savings by receiving data from the Home Finance server that stores information from parsed and categorozed Bank of America, Citizen and Discover statments.

Starter Kit User Guide

  • Start by clicking on the Balance link in the top level navigation bar to access the Complete Statistics page.
  • Next, follow on the right navigation bar to access the Complete Balance page in a choosen year and the Detail page of all trasactions made in a choosen month.
